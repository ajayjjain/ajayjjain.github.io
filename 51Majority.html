Writeup

A couple of interesting findings so far. The Democrats end up taking two seats from the Republicans: Nevada and Texas (which is a bit of a surprise given Ted Cruz's importance in the Republican Party). However, the Republicans gain more seats from the Democrats. Senator Heitkamp of North Dakota loses her seat to Representative Kevin Cramer (which would not shock political pundits if this does happen). In addition, Claire McCaskill (Missouri), Joe Donnelly (Indiana), and Sherrod Brown (Ohio) all lose their Senate seats to their respective Republican challengers. As a result, the Republicans gain one extra seat in the Senate.
